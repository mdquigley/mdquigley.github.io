Portfolio: For application to the NYU Steinhardt Master of Music in Music Technology program.

Mirror Music

Final project for Advanced Max/MSP/Jitter Programming with Professor Dafna Naphtali.

Summary: Mirror Music is a interactive synthesizer that generates random pitches within a user specified key, triggered by motion, and characterized in range and timbre by color.

FUNCH

Final project for Computer Music Synthesis with Professor Peter McCulloch.

Summary: A Max/MSP patch intended as a performance application with opportunities for collaboration between patch operator and live performer or prerecorded sound sources.

Visual Feedback In A Laptop Instrument

Final project for Psychology of Music with Professor Mary Farbood.

The "visual feedback" was based off of a system similar to that used in "Mirror Music" and the completed project acted as the preliminary research stage of my masters thesis.
